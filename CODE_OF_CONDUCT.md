# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive in all discussions.
- Keep issues, pull requests, and comments focused and actionable.
- Follow the project's technical conventions and communication style as documented in `AGENTS.md` and `CONTRIBUTING.md`.
- High-signal contributions only. Low-effort, dismissive, or off-topic behavior is not welcome.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in warnings, removal of contributions, or temporary/permanent bans at the discretion of the maintainer.

**Contact:** Report privately via GitHub issues or https://github.com/GabrielTenma
